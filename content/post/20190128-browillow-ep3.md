## Description

In this video, I further prepare us to talk about controversial topics by going over five rules to follow when you disagree with someone. 

The rules are:

1. Stay Calm
2. Choose Your Battles
3. Don't Make It Personal
4. Build a Steel Man
5. Keep an Open Mind
